# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview
Analyzed a retail superstore dataset to identify sales trends, 
category performance, and regional patterns using Power BI.
Built an interactive dashboard to support business performance 
reporting and data-driven decision making.

## Tool Used
- Power BI Desktop

## Dataset
- Superstore Sales Dataset — Kaggle (by Rohit Sahoo)
- 9,000+ rows of US retail transaction data

## Dashboard Features
- Total Sales KPI Card
- Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
- Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
- Sales by Region (Bar Chart)
- Interactive Segment Slicer (Consumer / Corporate / Home Office)

## Key Insights
- Technology was the highest revenue generating category at $0.83M
- West region outperformed all other regions in total sales at $0.71M
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of overall sales
- November recorded peak monthly sales at $0.35M
- Sales were consistently low from January to August then spiked 
  strongly from September onward indicating clear seasonal demand
